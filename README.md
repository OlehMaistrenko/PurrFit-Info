## Frequently Asked Questions

### The timer has no sound
Please check that:
- Your device volume is turned on
- Silent mode is disabled
- The app has permission to play sounds

### Can I customize workouts?
Yes. You can create and edit your own training sessions with custom work and rest intervals.

### Does the app require internet?
No. **PurrFit Timer works completely offline.**

### Does the app collect any data?
No. PurrFit Timer does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
