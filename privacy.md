# Privacy Policy for PurrFit Timer

**Effective date:** March 13, 2026

PurrFit Timer was developed by **Oleh Maistrenko** and is provided as a free mobile application.

This page explains how the app handles user information.

---

## Information Collection and Use

**PurrFit Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The application works entirely on your device and does not require:

- account registration  
- internet connection  
- personal data  

All timer settings and training configurations are stored **locally on your device**.

---

## Location Data

PurrFit Timer **does not collect precise or approximate location information** from your device.

---

## Third-Party Services

The application **does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies**.

No data is shared with external services.

---

## Children’s Privacy

PurrFit Timer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since the app does not collect any data, no personal information from children can be stored or processed.

---

## Security

Because PurrFit Timer does not collect or transmit user data, there is **no personal information stored on external servers**.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
